Takasima Jiro

5 stars chef from Kyoto

Takasima Jiro, an iconic figure in Japanese gastronomy, has established himself as a true master of Japanese culinary arts. Internationally renowned for his exacting standards and talent, he has dedicated his life to elevating the traditional flavors of his country. Each dish is a true work of art, crafted with the freshest seasonal ingredients and unparalleled technique. Takasima Jiro's philosophy is rooted in a constant pursuit of perfection. He views every meal as an opportunity to share his passion with his customers and introduce them to the subtleties of Japanese cuisine.

Makino Shiori

Michelin awarded chef from Tokyo

Makino Shiori, an emerging talent on the Japanese culinary scene, has swiftly captured the hearts of food lovers worldwide. Recognized by the prestigious Michelin Guide, he embodies the new generation of Japanese chefs who are pushing the boundaries of tradition while paying homage to it. With a masterful command of traditional techniques and boundless creativity, he reinvents Japanese culinary classics, adding his own unique touch. Carefully selected ingredients from local producers are elevated through bold flavor combinations and expertly executed cooking methods. Shiori Makino's philosophy is rooted in a delicate balance of tradition and modernity. He draws inspiration from ancestral recipes while adapting them to the tastes and expectations of discerning clientele. Each dish is a story, an emotion, an invitation to discovery

Takeshita Mamoru

Takeshita Mamoru is an exceptional Japanese chef, recognized worldwide for his expertise in kaiseki cuisine.
